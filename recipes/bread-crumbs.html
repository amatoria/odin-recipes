Bread Crumbs

Description

Homemade bread crumbs made with leftover bread. Used as a crunchy topping, coating, or mix-in for dishes such as breaded chicken, casseroles, and meatballs.

Ingredients

  • Any type of bread, cubed

Steps

  1. Set the oven rack in the center position. Preheat to 300 degrees F.
  2. Add cubed bread to food processor. For coarse crumbs, process on high speed for 30 seconds. For fine crumbs, process on high speed for 1 minute.
  3. Transfer into one large sheet pan or two small pans. Spread evenly in a layer in the pan.
  4. Bake for 5 minutes. Stir and bake for another 5 minutes. The bread crumbs should be lightly browned and feel dry to the touch.
  5. Bake for another 2 to 5 minutes until completely dry.
  6. Allow bread crumbs to cool for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Transfer to airtight container.
