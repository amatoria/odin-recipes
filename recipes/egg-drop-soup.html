Egg Drop Soup

Description

A Chinese soup consisting of beaten eggs poured over chicken broth. Often consists of black and/or white pepper and garnished with scallions.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/8 teaspooon white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch, mixed with 1/3 cup water
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 scallion
  • 1/4 teaspoon MSG

Steps

  1. In a medium stock pot, bring the chicken stock to a simmer.
  2. Stir in sesame oil, salt, sugar, white pepper, and MSG. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  3. Drizzle the cornstarch and water mixture into the soup, stirring the soup consistently as the mixture is added.
  4. Add the beaten egg to the soup, while stirring the soup consistently.
  5. Ladle the soup into bowls, garnish with scallions, and serve.
